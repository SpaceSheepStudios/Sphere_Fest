About Sphere Fest


What is Sphere Fest?


Sphere Fest is the world's biggest technology festival.
It is a platform for tech enthusiasts to come together
and share their knowledge.
The festival is a 3-day event that includes
workshops, talks, networking opportunities
and so much more.

Sphere Fest is a celebration of technology and innovation.
It aims to inspire and educate the next generation
of tech leaders.


Join them!

What can I do at the Festival?


In our Workshops, you can learn about the latest technologies and trends in the tech industry.
And the best part? You can experience them hands-on!

Our workshops are designed to be
interactive and engaging, for both beginners and experts.


Interested? Get your Ticket here!

Who will be there?


The talks at Sphere Fest are given by industry experts, visionaries
and thought leaders.
They cover a wide range of topics,
from AI and Machine Learning to Cybersecurity and Blockchain.

The talks are designed to be informative and inspiring,
to help you stay ahead of the curve in the tech industry.
